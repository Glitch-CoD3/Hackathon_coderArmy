What is a form in HTML?

  • A form is used to collect user input on a webpage.
  • It contains different fields like text boxes, radio buttons, checkboxes, dropdowns, and buttons.
  • After filling, the data can be sent to a server for processing (like login, signup, search, or feedback).
  • Without forms, websites cannot take input from users.
